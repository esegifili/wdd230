Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Aunu'u Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Aunu'u Chamber of Commerce aims to bring together locally owned businesses to promote and advocate their interest. In addition, through networking and collaborating business environments can be improved and communities are strengthened.

Scenarios

  1. What events are happening in the community?
  2. Where can I find contact information for local businesses?
  3. What benefits are there for joining the chamber of commerce and how do I join?

Color Schema

My main color that I will be using for background is the dark green, with the other shades of green and blues to highlight different elements on the page. The brown will be used for call to action buttons and other elements that I want to really stand out. Texts in the body will be either white or black.

Typography

I will be using two different kinds of fonts from Google, called Oswald and Lato. Lato will be used mainly for body text and Oswald for headings.
